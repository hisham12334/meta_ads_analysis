## Judge Critique

What failed:

- Hook is clear but generic.
- Does not mention the strongest offer: free shipping.
- Does not explain QADR's meaning or why the hoodie is emotionally different.
- Business value is directionally tied to stock clearance, but not strongly enough.

What to improve:

- Lead with real scarcity plus free shipping.
- Add the QADR meaning angle.
- Keep the copy short enough for Meta.
- Avoid fake urgency or unsupported claims.

What must not change:

- Do not invent testimonials.
- Do not claim premium quality unless product details support it.
- Do not recommend a bigger budget because stock is low.

## Regeneration Instruction

Rewrite the output using the critique.

Constraints:
- Keep claims verifiable.
- Keep the offer clear.
- Use the competitor-informed angle.
- Improve the first 125 characters.
- Do not change budget or publish anything without owner approval.
