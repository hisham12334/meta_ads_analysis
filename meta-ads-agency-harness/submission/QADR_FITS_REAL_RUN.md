## Strategy Produced Before Launch

Campaign:
- Objective: Sales
- Conversion location: Website
- Optimization event: Purchase if Pixel works, otherwise AddToCart
- Budget: lifetime budget INR 1,300
- Schedule: May 3, 2026 to May 5, 2026, 11:50 PM IST
- Location: India
- Age: 18-34
- Gender: all
- Placements: Advantage+ placements
- CTA: Shop Now

Naming:
- Campaign: `META_Sales_India_QADR-Hoodie_Last9Stock_May2026`
- Ad set: `India_18-34_Broad_WebsitePurchase_Last9`

Creative plan:
- 3 ads only because budget was small
- Front-facing hoodie photo
- Model wearing hoodie
- Short 7-12 second product video or reel

Offer decision:
- Use "Free Shipping + Limited 9 Pieces Only"
- Avoid heavy discounting because stock was low and the product was a single hoodie
- Backup offer: INR 100 off launch drop using code `QADR100`

Primary angle:

"Only 9 QADR hoodies left. Free shipping on this limited drop. Once this stock is gone, the drop closes."

## Why This Is A Good Business Choice

This business is narrow enough for the capstone:
- One store
- One product
- One market
- One small budget
- One real inventory constraint
- One clear business outcome: sell remaining stock or prove demand

It also demonstrates judgment:
- The agent did not overbuild the campaign.
- It did not recommend a large discount.
- It tied budget and schedule to stock risk.
- It used scarcity only because real stock was limited.
- It recommended sales objective instead of vanity traffic.

## Proof Interpretation

Honest proof:
- The workflow helped launch a real Meta Sales campaign.
- The campaign produced measurable purchase intent: 8 add-to-carts, 3 initiated checkouts, and 1 add-payment-info event from INR 270.06 spend.
- The strongest creative angle was stock scarcity, matching the workflow recommendation.

What not to claim:
- Do not claim a sale unless Shopify/order evidence confirms it.
- Do not claim ROAS because Meta returned no purchase value or purchase event.
- Do not claim the campaign fully spent the INR 1,300 budget; verified spend was INR 270.06.

Guardrail lesson:
- The agent recommended stopping before May 6, 2026, ideally May 5 at 11:50 PM IST.
- Meta campaign/ad set details showed a configured stop/end time of May 6, 2026 at 22:00 UTC.
- Verified delivery data showed no spend after May 5, but the stop-time mismatch should be called out as a reason to encode schedule validation more strictly.
