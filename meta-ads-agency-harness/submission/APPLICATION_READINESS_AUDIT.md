## What To Submit

Use the project as:

"A Meta Ads creative intelligence harness for Shopify/DTC brands that connects brand intake, competitor research, offer strategy, Meta performance diagnostics, creative brief generation, spend pacing, and human approval guardrails."

Do not pitch it as:

"A fully autonomous ads agent that launches campaigns and spends money."

The second claim is weaker and riskier. The first claim is true and aligned with the brief.
