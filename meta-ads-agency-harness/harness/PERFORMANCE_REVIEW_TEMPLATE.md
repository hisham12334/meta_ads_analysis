# Performance Review Template

## Purpose

Use this after the Meta Ads MCP returns performance data.

The review should separate:

- What happened.
- Why it may have happened.
- What we recommend.
- What requires owner approval.

## Review Inputs

Required:

- Date range.
- Spend.
- Purchases.
- Revenue or purchase value.
- Cost per purchase.
- ROAS.
- CTR.
- CPM.
- Frequency.
- Campaign, ad set, and ad breakdowns where available.

Helpful:

- Product margin.
- Target CPA.
- Target ROAS.
- Current inventory.
- Recent store conversion rate.
- Recent creative changes.
- Recent offer changes.

## Decision Rules

### Scale Candidate

Recommend scale only when:

- ROAS is above target.
- Cost per purchase is below target.
- Spend is high enough to judge.
- Purchase volume is not too thin.
- Inventory can handle more sales.
- Tracking confidence is acceptable.

### Pause Candidate

Recommend pause or serious review when:

- Spend exceeds the agreed test threshold.
- Purchases are zero or too expensive.
- CTR is weak and CPM is high.
- ROAS is below target with enough data.
- The owner cannot support more testing budget.

### Keep Learning

Recommend waiting when:

- Spend is too low to judge.
- Campaign is still in early learning.
- Purchase volume is thin but leading indicators are healthy.
- Recent edits make the data unstable.

### Creative Refresh

Recommend creative refresh when:

- Frequency is rising.
- CTR is declining.
- CPM is increasing.
- The same angle has been shown for too long.
- Comments or engagement suggest fatigue or confusion.

## Language Rules

- Say "recommend", not "must", unless tracking or spend safety is at risk.
- Explain confidence level.
- Mention attribution limitations.
- Never imply Meta or Shopify data is perfectly exact.
- Never execute a live change inside a review.
