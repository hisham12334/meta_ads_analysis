# Strategy Output Template

## Purpose

Use this after intake, market research, competitor analysis, offer extraction, and the local `100m-offers` skill.

The output should feel like a concise agency strategy memo for an ecommerce owner. It should be practical enough to execute, not just inspirational.

## Required Offer Inputs

Before writing the strategy memo, apply:

- `offer-extraction` for outcome, value, tension, and dominant offer.
- `skills/100m-offers-skill/SKILL.md` for Grand Slam Offer structure.
- `skills/100m-offers-skill/references/hormozi-frameworks.md` when the offer needs deeper work on niche, value stack, guarantee, scarcity, urgency, or naming.

The offer section must include:

- Specific avatar.
- Dream outcome.
- Core mechanism.
- Main objection or nightmare avoided.
- Value equation diagnosis:
  - Dream outcome.
  - Perceived likelihood of achievement.
  - Time delay.
  - Effort and sacrifice.
- Value stack.
- Guarantee or risk reversal.
- Real urgency or scarcity, if available.
- Three offer name options.

## Rules

- Be specific.
- Tie recommendations to margin, budget, and current readiness.
- Mark assumptions clearly.
- Do not promise guaranteed ROAS.
- Do not use fake urgency or fake scarcity.
- Do not create a value stack with made-up bonuses unless clearly labeled as proposed.
- Do not recommend scaling if tracking is broken.
- Do not recommend launching if the store cannot convert mobile traffic.
- If data is missing, say what is missing and why it matters.
